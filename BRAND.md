# Sommet Pro — Brand Identity
*Version 1.0 — April 2026*

---

## Brand Positioning

**Tagline (bilingual):** 
- FR: *"L'expertise locale, le service professionnel."*
- EN: *"Local expertise. Professional service."*

**Brand voice:** Confident, warm, no-nonsense. Speaks like a trusted neighbour who also happens to be very good at their job. Never corporate, never sloppy.

**Target customer:** Homeowners in Lachute, Grenville, Brownsburg, Saint-André area. Mix of permanent residents (30-60) and cottage/chalet owners. Values reliability, quality work, and not having to chase someone down for a call back.

## Visual Identity

### Colour Palette
| Role | Colour | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary | Deep Forest Green | #2D4A3E |
| Secondary | Warm Stone | #C4A882 |
| Accent | Charcoal | #2C2C2C |
| Background | Off-White / Cream | #F7F4EF |
| Text | Near Black | #1A1A1A |

### Typography
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (serif) — premium, editorial feel
- **Body / UI:** Inter or Lato (clean sans-serif)
- **Tagline:** Playfair Display Italic

### Logo Concept
- Wordmark: **SOMMET PRO** in Playfair Display, deep forest green
- Icon: Stylized mountain peak (sommet = summit) — simple, 2-line geometric peak
- Works well on white, dark green, or kraft paper backgrounds
- Truck-door friendly: horizontal lockup with icon left of wordmark

### Brand Elements
- Subtle texture: light wood grain or stone texture in backgrounds
- Photography style: real people, real work, warm light, Laurentians landscape in background
- No stock photo corporate vibes
