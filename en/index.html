About

Sommet Pro — Your Local Partner

Sommet Pro is a local professional services company based in Lachute, in the Laurentians region of Quebec. Founded to serve the needs of homeowners in the Argenteuil area, Sommet Pro offers four core services: residential landscaping, interior and exterior painting, home renovation, and small engine repair (lawnmowers, snowblowers, chainsaws, generators).

Our service area includes Lachute, Grenville-sur-la-Rouge, Brownsburg-Chatham, Saint-André-d'Argenteuil, and surrounding municipalities in the Laurentians. Sommet Pro stands out with fast response times (under 2 hours), quality workmanship, and deep roots in the local community.
